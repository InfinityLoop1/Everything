Battle of Lexington and Concord


Summary

The battle of Lexington and Concord was the first battle in the American Revolutionary War. The British marched into Lexington and Concord on April 19th. They had planned to seize American weapons and ammunition located in Concord, however the Americans fought back, and won, creating the Amerian Revolutionary War.

Casualties

Casualties are estimated to be 93 Americans and 300 British.
